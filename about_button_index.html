About ArqueoloGo

We are a robotics team made up of 7 members between 12 and 15 years of age who attend the IMAKE advanced robotics workshop. Our official name is Saint Juana de los Sushis Dorados (SJSD), the name arose from a half-distorted brainstorm where we got hungry. We invite you to learn by playing which archaeological pieces should not be removed from their original site and thus help archaeologists
